Frequently Asked Questions

How long can my essay be?
We accept submissions of up to 10,000 characters. If you have an essay that you would like reviewed that exceeds this character count, please email us at contact@thefatenvelope.org.
What details should I submit with my essay?
We strongly recommend that you send the prompt and college to which your essay is responding. In addition, feel free to submit any questions or concerns to which you feel your advisor should pay special attention while reading your essay.
What happens after I submit my essay?
We pair you with the essay advisor who best relates to your essay and life experiences. They rigorously analyze your essay to determine how well you have answered the prompt, how well you represent yourself, and how well you have written your narrative, among many other considerations. Your advisor will then send you an email with suggestions on how to best proceed with your essay.
What is the turnaround time for your services?
All of our regular services have a turnaround time of three days, starting from the moment of your essay or application submission. Expedited services have a 24-hour turnaround time, which also begins from the moment of your submission.
Will colleges know that I have used your services?
No, all client information is kept strictly confidential. For security purposes, we also advise you to remove all personal information from your application review submission. Most importantly, the final product of our services, whether an essay or an application, will be a unique and genuine reflection of you.
What forms of payment do you accept?
We accept most major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, etc.).
